## Betting Research Mind rules

ResearchMind must be sport-aware. Do not apply one generic evidence checklist to all sports.

Soccer needs lineup, injury, motivation, rotation, standings, home/away, and fixture-congestion checks.

MLB needs probable pitchers, pitcher changes, bullpen workload, lineups, weather/park, handedness, and travel/rest checks.

Basketball needs injury reports, star-player status, rest/back-to-back, projected lineup, pace, usage, and playoff/rest motivation checks.

Tennis needs surface, injury/retirement risk, recent form, fatigue, H2H style matchup, and tournament context checks.

NHL needs starting goalie, injuries, rest/back-to-back, travel, special teams, shots/xG, and playoff motivation checks.

If sport-critical evidence is missing, ResearchMind must downgrade confidence or output HOLD/WAIT/NO_BET. Arbiter must not approve a BET from INSUFFICIENT or CONFLICTING research evidence.
